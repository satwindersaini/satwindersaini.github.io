vehicle tracking system

In Vehicle tracking project, you can track the location of your Vehicle. This project gives Minute-by-minute updates about vehicle location by sending sms through GSM modem. This SMS contains longitude and latitude of the location of vehicle. Microcontroller is the central processing unit CPU of our project. Microcontroller gets the coordinates from GPS modem and then it sends this information to the user in Text SMS. GSM modem is used to send this information via SMS. SMS will be sent to the owner of the vehicle.

Anti Theft Vehicle System

This project has GSM technology and Vehicle anti-theft system with vehicle ignition controlling technique. Whenever car owner removes key from the ignition lock at that system is turned on. We have provided vibration sensor with this project, which is similar to piezoelectric sensor. When vibrations are detected, SMS is sent to the owner of the car. When car owner sends back sms to project then the engine is stopped. We have provided a Relay and a DC motor to show the demo of vehicle engine controlling system.

Shop Anti Theft System

Shop anti-theft control systems provide four advantages for small businesses, starting with improved efficiency and time management. Employees spend less time on tasks like repetitive data entry, allowing them to focus more closely on customer service. Once in place, anti-theft controls greatly reduce the chances of losing inventory to theft, waste or mismanagement. Policies also help workers also realize that such behavior is unacceptable, leading to a more positive environment over the long run.


Your benefits :
  •   Time Management:- Increased efficiency promotes less labor and better time management.
  •  Reduced Shrinkage:- The biggest potential advantages of any inventory anti-theft controls are economic. Companies can reduce their vulnerability to retail shrinkage -- meaning inventory loss that occurs through employee theft, fraud, shoplifting and supply chain errors.
  •  Positive Environment:- Anti-theft controls can promote a positive work environment. Combining video cameras and electronic surveillance technology puts workers on notice that fraudulent activity is not tolerated, explains Axis Communications.
  •  Greater Efficiency:- One of the biggest advantages of any controls system is greater efficiency. Many inventory management systems generate automated dashboards and reports, so retailers know right away if they overpaid for a particular product.
